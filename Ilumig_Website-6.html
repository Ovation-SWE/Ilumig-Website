Control and effective use of these resources are still evolving as humanities’ energy needs grow. We can bring the right people together and drive consensus starting with the folks in the plants, to the vendor for the plant controllers, to the Energy Management team, the network modeling team to figure out design and implementation to better use these resources on the grid.

With a deep knowledge of the utility space, the grid, collaboration, engineering and people, we can help you deal with the most complex of challenges with discipline, rigor and out of the box thinking. We hope you give us a chance.

Microgrids and other low voltage distributed Energy sources are getting more popular on the grid. Utilities are looking to use these types of resources with aggregation, AGC control, bid them on Energy markets, call on them for frequency response and much much more. We can help put the pieces of this puzzle together with you.

We can lead utilities through huge challenges due to lack of in-house knowledge whether it comes to how the substation with the battery resource is getting built, what the local controller is capable of, impacts of this resource on the grid, what points does operations need to read from these resources and what points does it need to control, what are the various modes that these units operate in, how the control systems need to be built, what is the modeling strategy and much more.
